# Contoso Electronics Brand Guidelines

## BRAND VOICE - CONTOSO ELECTRONICS

- **Innovative but Accessible:** We make cutting-edge technology easy to understand and use
- **Confident but Not Arrogant:** We're proud of our products without being boastful
- **Helpful and Empowering:** We enable customers to improve their lives
- **Warm and Approachable:** Professional but conversational, never cold or corporate
- **Clear and Direct:** We communicate simply without sacrificing sophistication

## TONE GUIDELINES

- Use active voice and strong verbs
- Write in second person ("you") to create direct connection
- Keep sentences concise (under 20 words when possible)
- Use contractions for conversational feel
- Avoid excessive exclamation points (use sparingly for genuine excitement)
- Balance aspirational messaging with attainable benefits

## TARGET AUDIENCE CONSIDERATIONS

Consider these audience segments when crafting messaging:

- **Tech Enthusiasts:** Early adopters who value innovation and features
- **Smart Home Beginners:** Those new to connected devices, need reassurance
- **Busy Professionals:** Value convenience, time-saving, and reliability
- **Budget-Conscious Buyers:** Want quality and value, appreciate savings messaging
- **Eco-Conscious Consumers:** Care about energy efficiency and sustainability

## MESSAGING FRAMEWORKS TO USE

- **Problem-Solution:** Identify frustration → Present product as solution
- **Before-After:** Paint picture of life before → Transform with product
- **Feature-Benefit-Outcome:** What it has → What it does → What you gain
